My Favourite Musician

MAX a.k.a Max Schneider

his wikipedia biography does not do him justice so I am going to write it myself. MAX was raised in Woodstock, New York. He began performing at a very early age. He acquired his first agent at the age of 14. In 2012 he got the lead role in a popular film, Rags. 3 Years later he released his first studio album with funding from Kickstarter. Now he is featured by many big artists like Galantis, Louis the Child, Matoma, Flux Pavillion.
